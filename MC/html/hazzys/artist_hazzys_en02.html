HAZZYS has launched 17FW HAZZYS X RAMDANE Capsule Collection together with RAMDANE TOUHAMI who is the owner and creative director of BULY 1803, a cosmetic brand loved by domestic celebrities. HAZZYS X RAMDANE Collection was the first Korean traditional brand that was displayed in the colette window of famous select shop in France from 31 July 2017 to 5 August 2017.


Located on the rue Saint-Honore street in the heart of Paris' luxury cultural center, ‘colette' has been steadily introducing products leading the world fashion trends every season since its opening in 1997. Colette is considered one of the top three editorial shops in the world, along with the US Opening Ceremony and Italy's 10CorsoComo, and is considered to be the beginning of a lifestyle select shop that has now become a fashion trend.

In particular, the 'Colette Show Window', which will be displayed on the entrance window of the shop by selecting one or two brands that 'colette' intends to introduce intensively every month, is regarded as a venue where the most influential products in the world are only displayed and the world famous designers’ collections have been displayed here.

In early September 2017, we have opened a HAZZYS pop-up store in the form of a complex cultural space combining clothing, beauty products and café in the Le Marais district of Paris which is called the heart of French fashion and arts and sell the first collection of ‘Artist Edition’.
