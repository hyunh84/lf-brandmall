BRAND HISTORY

HAZZYS started as a men's British traditional casual brand. Since then, it has expanded to various areas to Ladies, Accessories, Golf, Kids, etc., and proposes a lively look with a urban feel. HAZZYS' sophisticated casual style completes the style of modern people who enjoys work and leisure as well as travel and sports, and develops polished products with high-quality design through overseas partnership and outsourcing. Now, HAZZYS has grown beyond the domestic market to become a global brand loved even more by the global market.
