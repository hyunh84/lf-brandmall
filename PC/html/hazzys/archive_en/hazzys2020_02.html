THE HONOR
ANNIVERSARY
HAZZYS has broadened its reach toward the world after starting as a fashion brand in Seoul and widened its boundary to markets in Asia, Europe, and the U.S. Our journey has been successful and yielded positive response from the public. In the long time of twenty years, we now fully present new logo and pattern laid in front of the accomplishments we have made during the last two decades. English Pointer, a friend and a companion who walked alongside HAZZYS, has been reborn to have a natural look as if it had been hand drawn. Unnecessary details were boldly removed to create simple and precise shape which leaves long lasting impression. On top of everything, the new look wearing a stripe-patterned crown manifests courageous spirit. The sentiment is expressed by new stripe patterns designed based on yellow and navy and mixed with white and black. Distinctively woven bold lines drawn with three colors represents the bold and courageous sprit of HAZZYS while the moment these bold lines intersect with flexibility symbolizes the balancing characteristic of HAZZYS. These two logos and patterns are importance indices which embodies British Urbanity sought by HAZZYS in the past two decades.
