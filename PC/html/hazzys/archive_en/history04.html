ABOUT HAZZYS
HAZZYS which started as a British traditional casual brand for men have expanded its boundary to reach out to Ladies, Accessories, and Golf to manifest metropolitan and vibrant Urban British look. The refined casual style provided by HAZZYS whose heritage lies in sports brand of rowing have perfected the look of contemporary men busy with various activities including business meeting, outdoor activities, weekend trip, and other dynamic events. HAZZYS not only focused on styles but also shared its interest in culture and art. It discovered and sponsored many new creators from varying genres and launched outstanding collaboration with international artists. It also formed a community to introduce HAZZYS culture to clients and use it together to create yet another new culture. Now HAZZYS is taking another leap through excellent overseas outsourcing and partnership to become a global label and receive more love as a brand.
2016
HAZZYS Wimbledon Line Launched
HAZZYS signed an official partnership with the Wimbledon Championship and presented a collaboration collection for the first time in Korea. Wimbledon collection which consists of many types of shirts including pique shirts and t-shirts is created with using the best material optimized for performance to live up to the reputation of Wimbledon, the scene of the world’s best tennis matches.
2016
MR. HAZZYS, the HAZZYS MEN Business Suit Line
Mr. HAZZYS offers business suit line created with the top quality textiles imported from Italy and Japan. Centered around suits, the line offers an opportunity to create simple and formal mood with its shirts, jacket, trench coats. These suits are presented as a highly versatile business wear that can also be matched with more casual items such as t-shirts and sweaters to portray a harmoniously perfect fit.
2016 MR. HAZZYS, the HAZZYS MEN Business Suit Line
HAZZYS Launches in Vietnam
HAZZYS has signed an exclusive contract in Vietnam with KEI Trading Company Limited, a local fashion distribution company. Following the conclusion of the contract, HAZZYS opened up a flagship shop with the size of 100m2 on the 1st floor of Lotte Department Store Vietnam located in Hanoi which offers Mens, Ladies, and Accessory line all together. Also, HAZZYS Golf shop opened on 4th floor which exclusively offers golf clothing lines. With the opening of these two shops in Vietnam at once, HAZZYS let the world know of its successful opening of Vietnam branch. Supported by safely settling in China, HAZZYS accelerated its progress to spread out to South-East Asia market by entering Vietnam which is the largest market in the region.
2017 HAZZYS Launches in Vietnam
2017 HAZZYS Logo Renewed
17 years after the launch, HAZZYS has renewed its visual identity for the first time. As a part of a project for global business expansion, the new logo reflected modern design mood and changed luxurious crown and necklace on its symbolic English Pointer to more minimal design. Also, the font had been updated from serif type which either has slimmer and protruding edge or additional details to sans-serif style to emphasize modern style.
2018
HAZZYS MEN SKINCARE RULE429 Launched
RULE429 is the skincare line in HAZZYS MEN was launched by HAZZYS Digital Lab which observes and studies skin concerns and preferences of contemporary men. Referring to the result of various lifestyle surveys and big data analysis conducted on men living in urban environment, we have completed a exclusive formula and practical product line. The number ‘429’ refers to the men between the age of 29 to 49, and the number also stands for our goal to suggest special skincare techniques of HAZZYS. Not only does the skincare includes special recipe of British barbershops that use steam and walnut shells to remove dead skin layers but it also provides the signature scent of HAZZYS MEN concocted using English Herb, a unique skincare ingredient used by HAZZYS MEN.
2018
HAZZYS flagship store renewed as Space H in Myeongdong
HAZZYS flagship store in Myeongdong has been renewed as Space H and became the landmark of Asia. The building was designed by ‘Shin Ohori’, a renowned Japanese architect who also designed Fred Perry flagship store in Omotesando and Isabel Marant store in Kobe. It is a building that manifest the identity of HAZZYS perfectly and offers a space where visitors can experience various lifestyles ranging from fashion, art, books, café, and gardens all at the same time.
HAZZYS opens Ho Chi Minh branch in Vietnam
HAZZYS opened a new store in Diamond Department Store located in Ho Chi Minh. Adding 3 more stores since the beginning of its expansion into Vietnamese market by entering Lotte Department Store in Hanoi, HAZZYS has reinforced its position in Vietnam.
2019 HAZZYS opens Ho Chi Minh branch in Vietnam
2019
HAZZYS MEN 'RULE429' awarded with Excellent Industrial Design Product
RULE429, the skincare line of HAZZYS MEN, has been chosen as the ‘Good Design’ product in product design sector at ‘Good Design Award’ event hosted by Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Energy and supervised by Korea Institute of Design Promotion. ‘Good Design Award’ is a system that grants ‘Good Design’ mark for excellent products that has passed its comprehensive review on exterior design, functions, material, and economic efficiency meaning the package design of RULE429 has been approved for its competitiveness.
2019
HAZZYS enters London Fashion Week
2020 S/S collection created through the cooperation of Tim Coppens, the Global Creative Director of HAZZYS and renowned Chinese designer Haizhen Wang is ‘Back to London’ themed. The collection has been acclaimed for successfully expressing British cultural sentiment in a sophisticated and structural design by presenting long trench coat with bold length, jacket with asymmetric colors as a distinguishing point, and jumpsuit with loose fit all of which have been created by adding creativeness to traditional style. HAZZYS let the world know of its position as a top brand in Korea by becoming one of the few Korean brands to take part in London Fashion Week.
