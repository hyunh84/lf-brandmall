INNOVATION
HAZZYS has become a guideline one must bear in mind when discussing classical British style. The brand embodies premium materials and quality tailoring both of which are the foundation of the outfits worn by high-class British nobilities. On top of them, it delves deeper into the foundation and the root of British history to embellishes the unique, elegant, and exquisite details on its clothing. These details are often manifested as emblems, wappens, prints, and patterns and even becomes graphics and logos that continuously evolves in meticulous manner. The new beauty perfected by these series of evolution adds exclusive grace and class on HAZZYS. This is why we refer to HAZZYS as the brand that embodies the most British sentiment.
HARIATO
Global Creative Director Natasha DeMyere created a new patter ‘Hariato’ to celebrate the 20th anniversary of HAZZYS. Straight lines that connects horizontally, vertically, and diagonally resembling a thread being woven into a piece of textile means that the past, present, and future of HAZZYS is all connected. The ‘H’ placed at points where the lines intersect symbolizes the challenging spirit of HAZZYS dashing forward to grasp victory.

CHECK
Kilt and check patterns, Scottish traditional clothing, are never left out when discussing British legitimacy. Scottish tribes would draw the line between their territories by using the width and gaps between the lines forming tartan checks. As the example indicates, tartan check is the root of British people planted deep inside their lives. HAZZYS succeeds this tradition in modern style by laying colors of varying saturation over check patterns designed in dynamic width and gaps.

STRIPE
Stripe knit and t-shirts always existed in the scenes of leisure times enjoyed by British nobilities. Regardless of season or trends, they would wear stripe-patterned clothing when they were enjoying free time. As such, stripe was one of the symbolic patterns creating comforting and soothing atmosphere among the lives of the British nobilities. HAZZYS being the brand that always seeks to pursue British classic re-creates the stripe pattern to bear more boldness. The new stripe pattern is designed to have bolder blocks rather than those that are fine, and this new pattern embodies broad visions of HAZZYS within its prominent gaps.

PLAY GROUND
Pattern embodies many ideas. It holds the history and tradition of the generation that gave it a birth, and it also embodies the climate of its birth city, the language and much more unimaginable information. Since HAZZYS is a brand that prioritizes historic legitimacy more than any other brand, it tends to delve much deeper into patterns. More than anything, HAZZYS has been changing its logos and graphics using its flexible attitude. Amid the continuous development, emblems were also subjected to dynamic transformation and exerts surprising energy. Although the brand has its base built on tradition, the logos and emblems that reflect the flow of generations are the embellishment that act as an important media connecting the past, present, and future. Unexpected colors, patterns, and shapes form an exquisite balance.
