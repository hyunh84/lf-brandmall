ABOUT HAZZYS
HAZZYS which started as a British traditional casual brand for men have expanded its boundary to reach out to Ladies, Accessories, and Golf to manifest metropolitan and vibrant Urban British look. The refined casual style provided by HAZZYS whose heritage lies in sports brand of rowing have perfected the look of contemporary men busy with various activities including business meeting, outdoor activities, weekend trip, and other dynamic events. HAZZYS not only focused on styles but also shared its interest in culture and art. It discovered and sponsored many new creators from varying genres and launched outstanding collaboration with international artists. It also formed a community to introduce HAZZYS culture to clients and use it together to create yet another new culture. Now HAZZYS is taking another leap through excellent overseas outsourcing and partnership to become a global label and receive more love as a brand.
2011
HAZZYS opened flagship store in Myeongdong
HAZZYS flagship store in Myeongdong consists of 5 floors and total area of 1653 m2. Different brand heritages had been manifested in each floor ranging from rowing club, London art, and English Pointer. HAZZYS flagship store gives the visitors to see HAZZYS culture and allow them to share and experience this culture by offering an opportunity to browse through all HAZZYS lines including Men, Ladies, Accessory, and Golf in one spot.
2011 HAZZYS X UV, You Hee-Yeol, Jeong Jae-Hyung
UV, You Hee-Yeol, and Jeong Jae-Hyung meet with HAZZYS to create a branded music contents offering delightful sentiment. Jeong Jae-Hyung and HAZZYS got together and created a brand music contents offering delightful sentiment. The music video ‘Who am I’ which was filmed in U.K. armed with UV’s unique low comedy style along with the works of You Hee-Yeol and Jeong Jae-Hyung it was ranked at top in numbers of music charts. This music video featuring British sentiment created by 4 musicians along with their brilliant photo books were spiced up with each member’s unique charm to receive an explosive response from many audiences. Taking advantage of this success, HAZZYS was even ranked at the top of the most popular search engines including Naver and Nate.
2012
Launched London art collaboration items
The works of artist who participated in London Artist Residency Project and those selected through new artist contest were turned into HAZZYS fashion items ranging from t-shirts, pouch, and cups. This is a significant step as the artistic works of contemporary artist including Lee Ki-Il and Kim Ah-Young combined with their unique charm, identity, and British heritage were transformed into fashion items.
Launched 2012 HAZZYS London Olympic Limited Edition
Welcoming the opening of London Olympics, HAZZYS Golf launched a special celebratory golf wear line which offers items that reflect uniquely changed Union Jack pattern. HAZZYS Men launched a training line whose clothing has printings of main sports such as discus throw and cycles from 1908 London Olympics.

2012
HAZZYS Flagship in Myeongdong 10,000th Customer Event HAZZYS
HAZZYS Flagship in Myeongdong 10,000th Customer Event HAZZYS : Since its first establishment back in 2011, HAZZYS Flagshipstore in Myeongdong has been serving not only as a shopping hub but also as a complex cultural space where visitors can experience variety of brand and culture experience. HAZZYS culture club activities, artist drawing, music box live, and many other programs had been held to interact with customers. To celebrate the 10,000th customer at Myeongdong flagship store, we hosted various events including fan signing event featuring casts from ‘The Birth of an Amazing Star TOP3’.
2013
HAZZYS EYEWEAR Launched
HAZZYS launched ‘HAZZYS Eyewear 2013 Collection’ at its flagship store in Myeongdong. This eyewear line consists of 3 lines including technology, prestige, and vintage line. ‘Technology’ line especially caught many eyes by presenting innovative eyewear with ‘Groove Technology’ which is a new technology developed by HAZZYS Eyewear.
2013 HAZZYS Launches in Taiwan
HAZZYS became the first fashion brand in Korea to open up an independent shop in Taiwan. The first store opened up in the main branch of Pacific Sogo which is the largest department store in Taipei. All product offered in HAZZYS Taiwan is exported from Korea to maintain consistent design and quality of materials. The price range is also similar to that in Korea in order to abide by premium strategy
