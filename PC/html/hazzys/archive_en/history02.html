ABOUT HAZZYS
HAZZYS which started as a British traditional casual brand for men have expanded its boundary to reach out to Ladies, Accessories, and Golf to manifest metropolitan and vibrant Urban British look. The refined casual style provided by HAZZYS whose heritage lies in sports brand of rowing have perfected the look of contemporary men busy with various activities including business meeting, outdoor activities, weekend trip, and other dynamic events. HAZZYS not only focused on styles but also shared its interest in culture and art. It discovered and sponsored many new creators from varying genres and launched outstanding collaboration with international artists. It also formed a community to introduce HAZZYS culture to clients and use it together to create yet another new culture. Now HAZZYS is taking another leap through excellent overseas outsourcing and partnership to become a global label and receive more love as a brand.
2007
HAZZYS Expands to China
The first HAZZYS store in China opened up on September 10th, 2007. After signing a license contract with ‘Saint Angelo Group’ which owns top three men’s wear labels, HAZZYS opened up 5~6 more stores in major cities of China starting from the 1st store in Wenzhou, followed by Shanghai, Beijing, and Nanjing. HAZZYS Wenzhou is about 3558 ft2 and located in Color Plaza, the shopping hub in Wenzhou.
2007 HAZZYS Expands to China
2007
CULTURE CLUB Begins
People who live the brand gathered at HAZZYS Culture Club. Culture Club is a special community where the brand and consumers explore and consume the cultural value of HAZZYS. It allowed people to experience the brand heritage and take part in dynamic cultural events.
HAZZYS ACCESSORIES Launched
As HAZZYS Accessory launched as a separate label, HAZZYS let the world know of its start as a total brand equipped with Men’s, Ladies’, and Accessory line. This expanded line labeled as HAZZYS Accessory offers classic and modernized urban bags as well as small goods, all of which retain British heritage and classic style.
2007 HAZZYS ACCESSORIES Launched
2009
HAZZYS GOLF Launched
HAZZYS Golf showed clothes which kept the traditional British heritage while emphasizing the performance as golf wear. ‘Magic Series’ t-shirt is the main piece of HAZZYS Golf which is created to provide the wear a perfect swing experience by using high-tech performance material and considering the 3 dimensional movement of golfers body.
2009
HAZZYS Cambridge Rowing Club Launched
Celebrating its 10th anniversary since the launch, it formed an official partnership with 5 rowing clubs in Cambridge University including Churchill College Boat Club, Newnham College Boat Club, and Peterhouse Boat Club in order to reinforce its brand identity as a ‘Traditional Brisith Casual.’ ‘Cambridge Rowing Club’ line uses the arms, numbers, phrases, and colors which represent each club, and it offers jackets, shirts, sweaters, and pants that embodies the spirit of challenge and honor of rowing sports.
2010 HAZZYS MEN Shoes Line Launched
The first shoes introduced by HAZZYS satiated stylish men’s particular needs. HAZZYS MEN’s shoes line introduced 2 types of versatile leather loafers that can be worn with classic-formal look and elegant casual look as well as 2 types of sneakers giving more lively and sportive energy. All of these footwears are accessorized with English Pointer symbol and emblem.
2010 HAZZYS MEN Shoes Line Launched
2010
HAZZYS MEN Opened Mokdong Branch in Hyundai Department Store
HAZZYS opened its first flagship store in Hyundai Department Store in Mokdong. This store offers a total coordination with its premium line showing clothing made with top-quality materials from overseas such as wool containing cashmere and supima cotton, new shoes line, and additional leather goods imported from Italy.
2010 HAZZYS MEN Opened Mokdong Branch in Hyundai Department Store
2010
Hosted Contest for New Artist
HAZZYS hosted a content for new artist whose goal is to discover and sponsor new artists from varying sectors ranging from fine arts, design, photography, illustration, installation art, architecture, and pop art. The winner of the contest was offered with an opportunity to use ‘HAZZYS Studio’ located in Arlington House in U.K. for 3 months with all expenses for artistic activities and other cost covered by HAZZYS. The 2nd place was given a chance to go on culture tour in London, and 3rd place was gifted with HAZZYS clothing exchange coupons worth 1,000,000 Won. The contest was hosted in 2011 and 2012, and the selected artistic works were exhibited in Sejong Cultural Center in 2012.
