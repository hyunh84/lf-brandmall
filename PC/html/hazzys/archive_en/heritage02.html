ENGLISH POINTER
English Pointer, the symbol of HAZZYS, is a type of hunting breed of dog that accompanied the British nobilities when they went off for hunting. This breed is the best friend and a companion for the British people, and the British noble family of Edwards also has this breed engraved in their coat of arms. The breed’s name originates from its habit of lifting one paw to point toward the prey they spotted. The symbol ‘h’ represents the body shape of this particular breed with its head held high, elegantly arched back, and long straight legs. It has been dynamically reinterpreted as the symbol of HAZZYS.
